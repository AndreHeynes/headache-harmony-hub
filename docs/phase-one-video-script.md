# Phase 1 Introduction Video Script

## Video Overview
- **Duration:** 4-5 minutes
- **Tone:** Warm, encouraging, professional
- **Style:** Mix of presenter (or voice-over) with animated graphics and text overlays

---

## Scene 1: Welcome & Hook (0:00 - 0:30)

### Visual
- Fade in from black to a calming gradient background
- Text overlay: "Phase 1: Understanding Your Starting Point"
- Animated icon of a compass or map pin

### Narration
> "Welcome to Phase 1 of your personalized headache recovery program. 
> 
> Over the next 7 days, you're going to learn more about your headaches than you ever have before. And that knowledge? It's the foundation for everything that comes next.
>
> Let's walk through exactly what you'll be doing and why it matters."

### On-Screen Text
- "7 Days"
- "Your Foundation for Recovery"

---

## Scene 2: The Purpose of Phase 1 (0:30 - 1:15)

### Visual
- Split screen showing:
  - Left: Person looking frustrated/confused (representing current state)
  - Right: Person looking confident/understanding (representing goal)
- Animated arrow showing transformation
- Icons appearing: magnifying glass, chart, lightbulb

### Narration
> "Think of Phase 1 as your personal headache investigation.
>
> Right now, you might feel like your headaches are random, unpredictable, or completely out of your control. That's actually very common—and it's exactly what we're going to change.
>
> By the end of this week, you'll have a clear picture of:
> - How your headaches are affecting your daily life
> - What patterns might be contributing to them
> - And most importantly, what YOUR specific baseline looks like
>
> This isn't about fixing anything yet. It's about understanding. Because you can't improve what you don't measure."

### On-Screen Text (appear one by one)
- "✓ Measure your headache impact"
- "✓ Identify patterns"
- "✓ Establish your baseline"

---

## Scene 3: Your Daily Tasks Explained (1:15 - 2:30)

### Visual
- Animated calendar showing 7 days
- Each day lights up as it's discussed
- Icons for each task type: clipboard (questionnaires), pen (tracking), checkmark (completion)

### Narration
> "Each day this week, you'll have specific tasks to complete. Don't worry—they're designed to be manageable, even on difficult days.
>
> **Day 1** is all about orientation. You're doing it right now! You'll read through the program guide and watch this video. Simple.
>
> **Days 2 through 7** introduce our validated assessment questionnaires. These are the same tools doctors and researchers use worldwide to understand headache conditions.
>
> You'll complete questionnaires like:
> - The **HIT-6**, which measures how headaches impact your daily life
> - The **MIDAS**, which looks at disability and lost time
> - The **PSFS**, which helps us understand YOUR specific limitations
>
> Don't let the names intimidate you. Each one takes just a few minutes, and the questions are straightforward—just honest answers about your experience."

### On-Screen Text
- Day 1: "Orientation" → Guide + Video
- Days 2-7: "Assessments" → Questionnaires
- Visual of questionnaire names with friendly icons

---

## Scene 4: The Questionnaires (2:30 - 3:15)

### Visual
- Animated questionnaire form appearing
- Progress bar filling up
- Checkmarks appearing as sections complete
- Reassuring green colors

### Narration
> "A quick word about the questionnaires: There are no right or wrong answers. 
>
> These tools are designed to capture YOUR unique experience. Some questions might feel repetitive, but each one gives us different insights.
>
> Here's what to expect:
> - Most questionnaires have 5 to 15 questions
> - You can save your progress and come back anytime
> - Your answers are completely private and secure
>
> The most important thing? Be honest. If your headaches are severely affecting you, say so. If they're mild, that's fine too. Accurate information leads to better outcomes."

### On-Screen Text
- "5-15 questions per assessment"
- "Save progress anytime"
- "Be honest—there are no wrong answers"

---

## Scene 5: Daily Tracking (3:15 - 3:45)

### Visual
- Phone or tablet showing headache tracking interface
- Simple icons: calendar, intensity scale, notes
- Animation of someone quickly logging an entry

### Narration
> "Alongside the questionnaires, we encourage you to start tracking your headaches daily.
>
> This doesn't have to be complicated. Just note:
> - Whether you had a headache
> - How severe it was
> - Any potential triggers you noticed—like stress, sleep, or certain foods
>
> You can use our built-in tracker or connect an app you already use. The key is consistency. Even logging 'no headache today' is valuable data."

### On-Screen Text
- "Track daily—even headache-free days"
- "Note intensity & triggers"
- "Consistency matters"

---

## Scene 6: What Happens After Phase 1 (3:45 - 4:15)

### Visual
- Animated timeline showing Phase 1 → Phase 2 → Phase 3 → Phase 4
- Phase 1 highlighted, then arrow pointing forward
- Preview glimpses of exercises and treatment approaches

### Narration
> "So what happens after this week?
>
> The data you gather in Phase 1 directly shapes your Phase 2 experience. We'll use your results to:
> - Identify which therapeutic exercises will work best for you
> - Understand your specific triggers and sensitivities
> - Create a truly personalized recovery plan
>
> Think of Phase 1 as laying the groundwork. The stronger your foundation, the better your results in the phases ahead."

### On-Screen Text
- "Phase 1 → Personalized Phase 2 Plan"
- "Your data = Your custom program"

---

## Scene 7: Encouragement & Call to Action (4:15 - 4:45)

### Visual
- Warm, encouraging imagery
- Progress circle showing "Day 1 Complete" or similar
- Button/prompt to continue to tasks

### Narration
> "You've already taken the most important step—you've started.
>
> This week might feel simple, but what you're doing matters. Every questionnaire you complete, every headache you log, brings you closer to understanding and relief.
>
> If you have any questions, our support team is here to help. Otherwise, let's get started. Your Phase 1 guide is waiting, and your first tasks are ready.
>
> Here's to your recovery journey. Let's do this together."

### On-Screen Text
- "You've started—that's what matters"
- "Ready? Let's begin →"

## Technical Specifications

- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (16:9)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Audio:** Clear voice-over, subtle background music (calming/uplifting)
- **Captions:** Required for accessibility
- **File Format:** MP4 (H.264)

---

## Brand Guidelines for Video

- **Primary Colors:** Use brand teal/blue for headings and accents
- **Typography:** Match app fonts (clean, readable sans-serif)
- **Tone:** Professional but warm; encouraging without being dismissive
- **Accessibility:** High contrast text, clear audio, captions

---

## Production Notes

1. Consider creating a shorter 90-second version for users who want a quick overview
2. Include chapter markers for easy navigation
3. End card should include clear CTA button to return to Phase 1 tasks
4. Background music should be subtle—voice-over is the priority
