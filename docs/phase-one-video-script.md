# Phase 1 Introduction Video Script

## Video Overview
- **Duration:** 2 minutes
- **Tone:** Warm, encouraging, professional
- **Style:** Voice-over with animated graphics and text overlays

---

## Scene 1: Welcome (0:00 - 0:20)

### Visual
- Fade in to calming gradient background
- Text overlay: "Phase 1: Understanding Your Starting Point"
- Animated compass icon

### Narration
> "Welcome to Phase 1 of your headache recovery program. Over the next 7 days, you'll discover what's driving your headaches—and that knowledge is the foundation for your recovery."

### On-Screen Text
- "7 Days to Understand Your Headaches"

---

## Scene 2: What You'll Do (0:20 - 0:55)

### Visual
- Animated 7-day calendar
- Icons for questionnaires and tracking

### Narration
> "Here's what to expect: Day 1 is orientation—you're doing it now. Days 2 through 7, you'll complete quick, validated questionnaires that doctors use worldwide. These measure your headache impact, disability, and personal limitations.
>
> Each takes just a few minutes. Answer honestly—there are no wrong answers."

### On-Screen Text
- "Day 1: Orientation"
- "Days 2-7: Quick Assessments"
- "5-10 minutes per day"

---

## Scene 3: Daily Tracking (0:55 - 1:20)

### Visual
- Simple tracking interface animation
- Intensity scale and trigger icons

### Narration
> "Also start tracking your headaches daily—just note whether you had one, how severe, and any triggers you noticed. Even logging 'no headache today' is valuable. Consistency is key."

### On-Screen Text
- "Track daily • Note intensity & triggers"

---

## Scene 4: Why It Matters (1:20 - 1:50)

### Visual
- Timeline: Phase 1 → Phase 2 → Phase 3 → Phase 4
- Arrow showing personalization

### Narration
> "Your Phase 1 data directly shapes your personalized treatment plan. We'll use it to select the right exercises and strategies for YOU. The better your foundation, the better your results."

### On-Screen Text
- "Your Data = Your Custom Program"

---

## Scene 5: Let's Begin (1:50 - 2:00)

### Visual
- "Day 1 Complete" progress indicator
- Call-to-action button

### Narration
> "You've already started—that's what matters. Your first tasks are ready. Let's do this together."

### On-Screen Text
- "Ready? Let's begin →"

---

## Technical Specifications

- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (16:9)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Audio:** Clear voice-over, subtle background music
- **Captions:** Required for accessibility
- **File Format:** MP4 (H.264)

---

## Production Notes

1. Tight pacing—no pauses longer than 1 second
2. Use dynamic text animations to maintain engagement
3. End card should include clear CTA to return to Phase 1 tasks
